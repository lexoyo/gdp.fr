Zucchero
Genre: Folk/World
ADD TO FAVORITES
SHARE

His husky voice and hits like "Senza una Donna", "Il Volo", "Menta e rosmarin", "o Diamante" and more recently "Baila Morena" have turned the singer-songwriter Zucchero into one of the most popular Italian artists. He has sold several million albums worldwide.

Zucchero came to the fore in 1987 with his fourth album Blues and went on to become an international superstar. Since then, he has made eight other albums and worked with, among others, Joe Cocker, Miles Davis, BB King, Sheryl Crow, Tom Jones, Paul Young, Sting, Luciano Pavarotti, Bono, Eric Clapton and Johnny Hallyday.

Three years after his last album Fly, Zucchero is back with a new work entitled Chocabeck, released on 22 January 2011. It features eleven critically-acclaimed tracks and is one of the best albums, if not the best album, of his career. This excellent offering, without a single false note, showcases more than ever Zucchero’s prime asset: his unique voice, both powerful and charged with emotion. It is a pure auditory feast, featuring premier league guests like Bono (U2) and Brian Wilson (The Beach Boys). 'Alla Fine' is the first track off Chocabeck to be released.

Zucchero est de retour cette année avec un nouvel album "Black Cat", sorti le 29 avril.
